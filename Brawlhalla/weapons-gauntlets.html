Gauntlets Attacks

Ground Attacks

  • - Neutral Light: The user delivers a series of rapid punches. If it lands successfully, a final blow follows that knocks the opponent away.
  • - Side Light: The user performs a short-range rising uppercut, sending opponents diagonally upward with low knockback, creating potential for follow-up attacks.
  • - Down Light: The user steps ahead and slams the ground with one hand. This move has a longer reach than most gauntlet attacks and causes low knockback, allowing for easy chaining into another move.

Air Attacks

  • - Neutral Air: The user clasps their hands and attempts to catch the enemy above. If caught, the opponent is slammed downward.
  • - Side Air: The user lunges forward with a punch. If it connects, a follow-up hit is triggered that sends the enemy flying.
  • - Down Air: The user throws a series of downward punches toward opponents below.

Specials

  • - Recovery: The user launches upward with a strong uppercut, sending enemies directly above them flying.
  • - Ground Pound: The user drops down with their arms pulled back. Upon hitting an enemy or cancelling the move, the user slams their fists downward. This ground pound can be steered slightly forward after activation.
