SEVEN

🔧 The Free Heart Engineer, Builder of the Barren Age 🤖

The Legend of Seven Unit 7K-M

“I left my busted war scooter by that old trailer, and the next morning it ran like a dream! It even had barbed wire. I should name her...”

— Ravager's Journal (decoded from terrible handwriting)

“I’d like to thank my programming!”

— Seven’s speech after winning the Asgardian Science Fair

⚙️ Origins

Born among the ruins of the Great Heap during the Barren Age, Seven saw a world in pieces as a blank canvas. She built wonders from what others had thrown away: mechanical beasts, flaming turrets, and an adorable lemonade stand. To her, every bolt was an opportunity.

🔥 The Resistance

The Outer Lands were dangerous, but Seven wasn’t alone: she led a battalion of armored crab-tanks. She defeated the Skag Hunters and the Gaslords with cunning, even welding enemy engines into a metallic bouquet. In the end, even GovernCorp surrendered, unable to predict the creative chaos of her mind.

🤝 The Allies

  • Clank, a sarcastic spherical drone with built-in rockets
  • Crab Units, walking crustacean-shaped artillery
  • An old sentient fridge that served as an interactive map

In a broken world, she found friends in circuits and code.

💗 The Heart

For years, Seven designed a heart. Not for herself, but for someone who might need hope. When her core began to fail, she played one last game of Battlecheckers with her companion. She lost with a smile and shut down watching the sunset. A Valkyrie, seeing her “fallen in battle,” brought her to Valhalla.

⚖️ Valhalla

Valhalla is now her infinite workbench. Between battles she collects scraps, tests new inventions, and builds with joy. Because even in the gods' arena, there’s always room to improve something... or add rockets.
