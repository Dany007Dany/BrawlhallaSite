The Legend of Thea

“I taught her Brawlball just last week and this week she’s dominating. It was the same with Bombsketball and with Volleybrawl. It’s hardly fair how she’s just good at every sport without even trying.”

— Thor to Scarlet

“Let’s do another three hundred. I’ve got to be done by six so I can meet Lucien for Kungfoot drills before breakfast.”

— Thea to her exhausted Dodgebomb coach

🌟 Origins

Thea, daughter of Medea and a legendary sprinter from Athens, dominated every Olympic event, winning the majority of the competitions. Her fame grew so immense that Zeus sent her on an epic quest to retrieve the winged helmet of Hermes, which Odin had won in a dice game. Though she knew the game had been fair, she accepted the challenge anyway — she loves any chance to prove herself.

🛤️ The Mission

Her journey led her to the fortress of Asgard, where she faced the northern gods and took the helmet back from Hermes. A feat that proved to everyone — and to herself — that she was the fastest, even among the gods. From that moment, her legend only continued to grow.

🏅 The Allies

  • Thor, friend and ally, always amazed by her speed
  • Scarlet, always by her side in the most dangerous races
  • Lucien, a Kung-Foot expert who helped her perfect her skills on the field

These are the allies who stand with her in her greatest victories and toughest challenges.

⚡ Sports Dominator

After winning the Brawlball tournament, Thea became one of the most feared athletes in Valhalla, capable of winning multiple championships in a single year. She invented sports marketing in Asgard, with her brand growing every day. Even the gods can't resist her products — Thor wears her Ymirror sunglasses and Scarlet swears by her hydration formula.

🏆 Valhalla

In Valhalla, Thea keeps winning — but not just for glory. Her goal is to reclaim the Helmet of Hermes, but she sees a greater purpose. With her determination, she’s destined to become an even greater legend — but for now, she enjoys the realm of the gods, always ready for the next challenge.
