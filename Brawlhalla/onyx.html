The Legend of Onyx

“You think you want the real gargoyle to guard your castle, but wait till you've had to clean up burglar.”

— Roddrick, Castellan of Batavia

“You'd think after all the hard work I do we wouldn't have intruders anymore, but I probably tear someone limb from limb every couple weeks!”

— Onyx

🔥 From the Depths

Onyx barely escaped the torment of the Infernal Realm. After climbing through abysses and eternal flames, she emerged into an unknown castle, pursued by demons determined to drag her back. She had no plan — only the strength of desperation.

🏰 The Oath

By binding herself magically to the castle’s stone, Onyx vowed to protect it. No power — not even infernal — could tear her from her duty. For a hundred years, she served as Batavia’s guardian, an unyielding bastion against any siege.

🛡️ The Battles

  • The War of the North, against the Ice Titans
  • The Siege of the Hundred Thousand Turks
  • The Battle of the Dragonriders

No wall fell under her stone gaze.

⚔️ The Betrayal

During the legendary Siege of the Lions, Onyx discovered the truth about the Mad King she had sworn to defend. Renouncing her post — knowing she would be dragged back to the Inferno — she let the castle fall. A Valkyrie, moved by her sacrifice, saved her in the nick of time.

✨ Valhalla

Now Onyx stands guard over Valhalla as her new fortress. She defends its gates with the same ferocity and fights in the tournaments to stay battle-ready. Hoping this may be her eternal refuge... and that she can finally finish this month’s book.
