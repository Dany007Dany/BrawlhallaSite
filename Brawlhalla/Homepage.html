What's Brawlhalla?

Brawlhalla is a free 2D platform fighting game developed by Blue Mammoth Games and published by Ubisoft. The game supports up to 8 players in online or local modes, with full cross-play compatibility between PC, PlayStation, Xbox, Nintendo Switch, iOS, and Android. Battles take place in floating arenas, where the goal is to knock opponents off the screen using a mix of light attacks, heavy attacks, and special moves. With over 100 million players, Brawlhalla stands out for its accessible yet deep gameplay, frequent updates, and a wide variety of game modes.

Who are the creators?

Blue Mammoth Games is a development studio based in Atlanta, founded in 2009 by Lincoln Hamilton and Matt Woomer. After the success of their first game, Dungeon Blitz, they launched Brawlhalla in 2014, which quickly became their flagship title. In 2018, Ubisoft acquired Blue Mammoth Games, enabling the studio to further expand the game and reach a global audience. Today, Blue Mammoth Games continues to lead the development of Brawlhalla, maintaining a close relationship with the community and delivering regular updates.

How is the game structured?

Brawlhalla offers a wide range of game modes to satisfy both competitive and casual players. The modes include:

  • Casual Matchmaking: unranked matches, ideal for fun without pressure.
  • Ranked: competitive mode with seasonal leaderboards.
  • Custom Online: private rooms to play with friends.
  • Brawl of the Week: weekly rotating special modes with unique rules.
  • Offline Play: local play against bots or other players.

The game features over 50 Legends, each with two distinct weapons and a unique playstyle. Weapons include swords, hammers, spears, blasters, and many more, offering a variety of combat styles. Legends can be unlocked using in-game currency or purchased directly.

Crossovers and updates

Brawlhalla is well known for its numerous crossovers with popular franchises, introducing characters and skins inspired by series such as Adventure Time, WWE, Street Fighter, Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles, Kung Fu Panda, and many more. These special events bring new game modes, themed maps, and exclusive content. The game receives regular updates that include balance changes, new Legends, seasonal events, and battle passes, keeping the gameplay experience fresh and engaging for the community.
