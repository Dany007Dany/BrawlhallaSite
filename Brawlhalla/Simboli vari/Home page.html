Cos'è Brawlhalla?

Brawlhalla è un picchiaduro platform 2D gratuito sviluppato da Blue Mammoth Games e pubblicato da Ubisoft. Il gioco supporta fino a 8 giocatori in modalità online o locale, con pieno supporto al cross-play tra PC, PlayStation, Xbox, Nintendo Switch, iOS e Android. I combattimenti si svolgono in arene sospese, dove l'obiettivo è lanciare gli avversari fuori dallo schermo utilizzando una combinazione di attacchi leggeri, pesanti e mosse speciali. Con oltre 100 milioni di giocatori, Brawlhalla si distingue per il suo gameplay accessibile ma profondo, aggiornamenti frequenti e una vasta gamma di modalità di gioco.

Chi sono i creatori?

Blue Mammoth Games è uno studio di sviluppo con sede ad Atlanta, fondato nel 2009 da Lincoln Hamilton e Matt Woomer. Dopo il successo del loro primo gioco, Dungeon Blitz, hanno lanciato Brawlhalla nel 2014, che è diventato rapidamente il loro titolo di punta. Nel 2018, Ubisoft ha acquisito Blue Mammoth Games, permettendo allo studio di espandere ulteriormente il gioco e raggiungere un pubblico globale. Oggi, Blue Mammoth Games continua a guidare lo sviluppo di Brawlhalla, mantenendo una stretta collaborazione con la community e implementando aggiornamenti regolari.

Com'è strutturato il gioco?

Brawlhalla offre una vasta gamma di modalità di gioco per soddisfare sia i giocatori competitivi che quelli casual. Le modalità includono:

  • Casual Matchmaking: partite non classificate, ideali per il divertimento senza pressione.
  • Ranked: modalità competitiva con classifiche stagionali.
  • Custom Online: stanze private per giocare con amici.
  • Brawl of the Week: modalità speciali a rotazione settimanale con regole uniche.
  • Offline Play: gioco locale contro bot o altri giocatori.

Il gioco presenta oltre 50 "Leggende", ciascuna con due armi distintive e uno stile di gioco unico. Le armi includono spade, martelli, lance, pistole e molte altre, offrendo una varietà di stili di combattimento. Le "Leggende" possono essere sbloccate attraverso la valuta di gioco o acquistate direttamente.

Crossover e aggiornamenti

Brawlhalla è noto per i suoi numerosi crossover con franchise popolari, che introducono personaggi e skin ispirati a serie come Adventure Time, WWE, Street Fighter, Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles, Kung Fu Panda e molti altri. Questi eventi speciali portano nuove modalità di gioco, mappe tematiche e contenuti esclusivi. Il gioco riceve aggiornamenti regolari che includono bilanciamenti, nuove "Leggende", eventi stagionali e pass battaglia, mantenendo l'esperienza di gioco fresca e coinvolgente per la community.

📜 Brawlhalla Lore

🌍 Places

🏔️ Asgard

Asgard is an afterlife location home to gods, based off of norse mythology. Asgard is connected to Brawlhalla as it is home to Valhalla, where all combat in Brawlhalla takes place.

🏟️ Valhalla

Valhalla is the main location where the battle takes place, and can be seen depicted on the map The Great Hall. This is based on the Norse mythological Valhalla, a great hall in Asgard for warriors who have died in combat. As such, most of the Legends of Valhalla have come here through death in combat, and are destined to fight forever. There are a few Legends who didn't die in combat and are still in Valhalla, however:

  • - Bödvar, who broke down the doors of Valhalla and entered himself, as he felt he would never be defeated in battle.
  • - Brynn, who was born in Valhalla.
  • - Orion, who mysteriously fell from the Asgardian sky.
  • - Ulgrim, who is one of The Sons of Ivaldi and has always been in Asgard.
  • - Kor, who was built by Elven sorcerers for combat against Asgard. Also, Kor has his job as a platform mover in Valhalla.
  • - Sir Roland, who was punched by the Valkyrie Hypernia as an old man on his deathbed, so he could technically die in battle.
  • - Thor, who is the son of Odin.
  • - Munin, who, along with her sister Hugin, are the messengers of Odin and thus live somewhere within Asgard.

🤝 Groups and Factions

MBFC

Abbreviated as MBFC, this organization is referenced in Ada and Val's lore, and suspiciously seems to work in nanotech (their slogan being "Your Friend in Nanotech"). Their logo can be seen in lots of places, such as Ada's shirt, Val's forehead, High Frequency Yumiko's headphones, and in the background of Miami Dome.

🛡️ Iron Legion

The Iron Legion is a mercenary army serving Azoth’s empire, originally led by the Troll Titan and later taken over by Xull, who made it wealthy and powerful. Xull recruited by placing a bounty on himself, offering survival and enlistment to those who could endure his attack. Teros was a known member and helped win the Battle of Orc Pass for Azoth. The Iron Legion is featured in skins for Commander Xull, Iron Legion Teros, and Iron Legion Mordex—though Mordex’s connection to the group is unconfirmed.

🎸 Electric Fight Orchestra

This "Techno-Fantasy Euro Pop" Band is mentioned in Isaiah's lore, and consists of Isaiah, Ada, Ulgrim, and Fait. This group has been referenced in both official art and in the Welcome to Brawlhalla animated short. The group's name was confirmed in a teaser for a new legend.

📚 Book Club

As the name implies, this is a ladies' book club in Valhalla. Confirmed members of the club are Scarlet (the group leader), Fait, and Onyx. Xull has also challenged Scarlet for leadership of the group (though he is not actually part of the group).

🦁 Exalted Lion

The Order of the Exalted Lion was a group of hunters who fought monsters in Batavia. Jhala was the first to be titled “Exalted Lioness” by Apollo after uniting Thera against Azoth. Diana and Mordex were later members, but the order was destroyed when Mordex, seeking power, took the Geminus Virens. This story is explored in Battle Pass Season 4, though set in different timelines. Queen Nai, Cross, and Azoth have skins from that season but are not members. Sir Roland was part of the order at an unknown time, and Isaiah is confirmed as the leader of its modern version.
