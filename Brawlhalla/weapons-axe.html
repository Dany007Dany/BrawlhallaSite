Axe Attacks

Ground Attacks

  • - Neutral Light: The user swings the axe horizontally. If it lands, the attack continues with a second blow that knocks the opponent away.
  • - Side Light: The user chops into the ground with their axe, launching the enemy upward. The knockback is minimal unless the opponent is already heavily damaged.
  • - Down Light: The user slashes the axe diagonally from below, sending the opponent flying. It’s a strong move that can combo into aerials.

Air Attacks

  • - Neutral Air: The user swings their axe in a wide arc above them, catching enemies in a broad range.
  • - Side Air: The user swings the axe horizontally with great force, launching enemies far. The final hit angle changes slightly at the end.
  • - Down Air: The user swings below themselves to strike enemies underneath. If close to the ground, they follow up with a backswing behind them.

Specials

  • - Recovery: The user leaps diagonally upward while spinning the axe around themselves. The longer the hit connects, the less damage and force it deals.
  • - Ground Pound: The user spins the axe around their body while falling straight down, hitting enemies caught in the motion.
